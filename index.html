Compliance, Responsibility, and Manufacturer Status

What is Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR)?

**Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR)** is a policy approach under which producers are given significant responsibility—financial and/or physical—for the treatment or disposal of post-consumer products. At Tex VCI, this means we are dedicated to managing the entire life cycle of our VCI packaging materials, driving towards circularity and minimal environmental impact.

**Why EPR Matters to Your Business:** Choosing an EPR-compliant manufacturer like Tex VCI reduces your regulatory burden, mitigates supply chain risk, and ensures your commitment to global sustainability standards, enhancing your brand's reputation.
